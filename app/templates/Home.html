What We Are

Our mission is to create a series of AI powered, interactive chatbots that simulate the conversational style of pop culture icons from movies and television. This interactive platform will allow anyone to enjoy the company of their favorite characters with the user controlling the flow of the interaction. We hope to create an engaging environment for people to converse with a variety of personalities and see where the conversation takes them.
